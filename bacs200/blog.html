Response to Pandemic

Social implications

The new crown epidemic is the awakening of history

and a concentrated manifestation of typical historical

scenes. Usually we are used to the surface of ordinary life,

but the storm of the epidemic has opened a historical perspective

to us. In some respects, the impact of the epidemic on human

society is short-term and temporary, but the chain reaction

it stimulates may be long-term.
