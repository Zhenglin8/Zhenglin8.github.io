Response to Pandemic

Social implications

The new crown epidemic is the awakening of history and a concentrated manifestation of typical historical scenes. Usually we are used to the surface of ordinary life, but the storm of the epidemic has opened a historical perspective to us. In some respects, the impact of the epidemic on human society is short-term and temporary, but the chain reactio it stimulates may be long-term.

Economic implications

When the new crown pneumonia has become a global pandemic, the extent of its impact on the global economy is also a major issue that worries the world.Reference is also defined as a global pandemic H1N1 influenza. According to the Bank of Mexico, it led to a reduction in GDP of approximately 5.3% in Mexico, where it first occurred, and an economic loss of tens of billions of dollars. Looking back at SARS, which has also spread to the world, according to statistics from the Brookings Institution, a think tank in the United States, SARS caused about 40 billion U.S. dollars in losses to the global economy that year. In contrast, the new crown virus is more cunning-it is more infectious than SARS and has a higher fatality rate than influenza, which makes the global impact of the epidemic more widespread.
