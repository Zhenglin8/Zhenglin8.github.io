About Us

At Windsor High School We believe that intellectual development, love of learning, and character strength are mutually reinforcing, equally important educational goals. With a firm commitment to ourcore values and rigorous academic programs, we prepare our students to live a life of purpose, achievement and generosity.

Program

We think the cultivation of students' character is as important as the education of intellectual growth. Under the guidance of values, students learn to use their wisdom and skills effectively to serve others.
